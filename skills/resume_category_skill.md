## Category Descriptions

### INFORMATION-TECHNOLOGY
IT resumes typically feature roles like IT Specialist, Director of IT, and Project Manager, with an emphasis on technical infrastructure and system management. Key skills commonly include Windows server administration, Active Directory, networking, and various enterprise software platforms like EPIC. Certifications and expertise in specific technologies are prominently featured, along with experience in team leadership and project management.

### BUSINESS-DEVELOPMENT
Business Development resumes showcase roles focused on revenue growth, market expansion, and client relationship management. These professionals typically have titles like Business Development Manager, Director, or Coordinator, with strong emphasis on sales, marketing, and strategic planning skills. Experience in negotiation, client relations, and project management are consistently highlighted, often accompanied by quantifiable achievements in revenue growth.

### ADVOCATE
Advocate resumes span various industries but commonly focus on customer service, healthcare, and social services roles. These positions typically require strong communication skills, empathy, and knowledge of relevant regulations or procedures like HIPAA. Experience in case management, conflict resolution, and client support are frequently highlighted skills.

### CHEF
Chef resumes emphasize culinary expertise, kitchen management, and menu development skills. However, the provided excerpts seem to contain mixed content not specifically related to culinary roles. Traditional chef resumes would typically highlight cooking techniques, kitchen operations, food safety certifications, and team management experience.

### FINANCE
Finance resumes feature roles such as Finance Director, Supervisor, and Operations Manager, emphasizing financial management and analysis skills. Key competencies typically include budget management, financial reporting, and experience with large-scale financial operations, often managing budgets in the millions. These professionals commonly highlight their experience with board presentations and strategic financial planning.

### ENGINEERING
Engineering resumes showcase technical expertise in specific engineering disciplines, often featuring advanced degrees and specialized technical skills. Common elements include experience with CAD software, project management tools, and technical analysis capabilities. Leadership roles often emphasize both technical expertise and team management experience, with many highlighting specific project achievements.

### ACCOUNTANT
Accountant resumes consistently emphasize experience in financial statement preparation, audit support, and general ledger management. These professionals typically highlight 10+ years of progressive experience, proficiency with accounting software, and expertise in multiple accounting functions. Strong analytical skills and deadline-focused work habits are commonly emphasized traits.

### FITNESS
Fitness industry resumes typically highlight certifications like CPR, First Aid, and specific fitness training credentials. Key skills include knowledge of exercise techniques, fitness assessments, and BMI calculations. Customer service abilities and experience with fitness equipment operation are commonly emphasized competencies.

### AVIATION
Aviation resumes typically feature roles like Aviation Management and Front Desk positions at airports or airlines, with education from specialized institutions like Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University. Key qualifications often include multilingual abilities, customer service skills, and specific aviation-related certifications or degrees. These resumes emphasize both technical aviation knowledge and administrative capabilities.

### SALES
Sales resumes consistently highlight customer service skills, revenue generation, and relationship building capabilities. Common positions include Sales Clerk, Sales Coordinator, and Senior Sales Assistant, with emphasis on metrics like sales targets and customer retention. These resumes typically showcase experience with inventory management, CRM systems, and demonstrated success in meeting sales goals.

### HEALTHCARE
Healthcare resumes feature strong clinical credentials, often including RN licensure or specific medical certifications. Key roles include Healthcare Recruiter, Occupational Health Nurse, and various clinical coordinator positions, with emphasis on patient care experience and medical terminology. These resumes typically highlight both technical medical skills and administrative capabilities in healthcare settings.

### CONSULTANT
Consultant resumes emphasize advisory expertise in specific domains, problem-solving abilities, and project management skills. They typically showcase strong analytical capabilities, interpersonal skills, and experience in developing strategic solutions. These resumes often highlight specific methodologies, certifications, and quantifiable results achieved for clients.

### BANKING
Banking resumes focus on analytical skills, financial software proficiency, and customer service capabilities. Common positions include Bank Operations Analyst and Assistant Manager, with emphasis on compliance knowledge and attention to detail. These resumes typically highlight experience with financial systems, regulatory requirements, and data analysis tools.

### CONSTRUCTION
Construction resumes emphasize project management experience, technical knowledge of building processes, and safety compliance. Key roles include Construction Manager and Project Manager, with focus on managing large teams and complex projects. These resumes typically highlight experience with budgeting, contractor management, and specific construction methodologies.

### PUBLIC-RELATIONS
PR resumes showcase strong communication skills, media relations experience, and campaign management abilities. They emphasize social media expertise, content strategy, and brand development capabilities. These resumes typically highlight successful PR campaigns, media placements, and demonstrated ability to manage corporate communications and public image.

### HR
HR resumes emphasize experience with employee relations, benefits administration, and HR compliance. Common positions include HR Generalist, HR Administrator, and HR Executive, with focus on HRIS systems and employment law knowledge. These resumes typically highlight experience with recruitment, employee development, and HR policy implementation.

### DESIGNER
Designer resumes typically include roles like Mechanical Designer, Information Designer, and Graphic Designer, with experience ranging from 3-28 years. Key skills focus on technical design principles, software systems, brand identity development, and project management capabilities. These professionals commonly highlight expertise in specific design software, leadership abilities, and both independent and team-based work approaches.

### ARTS
Arts-related resumes emphasize creative and educational roles, particularly in teaching and marketing contexts. These professionals typically possess strong communication skills and demonstrate experience in curriculum development or creative program implementation. Common themes include passion for student development, marketing automation expertise, and the ability to build relationships with diverse stakeholders.

### TEACHER
Teacher resumes consistently emphasize classroom management, curriculum development, and student engagement skills. They typically highlight certifications, experience with educational technology platforms, and demonstrated success in building motivated learning environments. These professionals often showcase their ability to work with diverse student populations and implement evidence-based teaching practices.

### APPAREL
Apparel industry resumes focus on product development, sourcing, and sales within the fashion/sportswear sector. Key competencies include global sourcing experience, technical design expertise, and understanding of manufacturing processes. These professionals often highlight international experience, trend analysis capabilities, and strong vendor relationship management skills.

### DIGITAL-MEDIA
Digital media professionals emphasize content creation, editing, and multi-platform communication skills. Their resumes typically highlight experience in both traditional and digital media, with strong focus on storytelling, brand management, and strategic communications. Technical proficiency in various media platforms and content management systems is consistently emphasized.

### AGRICULTURE
Agriculture-related resumes show diverse roles ranging from customer service to education and community involvement. These professionals often highlight experience with 4H programs, agricultural education, and community development. Key skills typically include project management, client service, and practical agricultural knowledge.

### AUTOMOBILE
Automobile industry resumes focus on service management and customer support roles. These professionals typically emphasize comprehensive industry knowledge, customer service excellence, and staff management capabilities. Key competencies include technical automotive expertise and ability to resolve customer service issues efficiently.

### BPO
BPO resumes emphasize service delivery, operations management, and workforce administration roles. These professionals typically showcase experience in managing large teams, implementing process improvements, and maintaining client relationships. Key skills include operational efficiency, customer service excellence, and administrative expertise.
